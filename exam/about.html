About Us

Who We Are

Exam Quiz is an educational platform created to help students prepare for competitive examinations such as TNPSC, UPSC, Group Exams, TNUSRB and Tamil Nadu school syllabus examinations.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide free, high-quality, exam-oriented quizzes that help students practice effectively, improve accuracy, and gain confidence.

What We Offer

  • Subject-wise & topic-wise quizzes
  • Current Affairs (Daily, Monthly, Yearly)
  • Exam-oriented MCQs with explanations
  • Mobile-friendly practice platform

Content Source

Questions are prepared based on standard textbooks, government publications, previous year questions, and reliable public sources.
